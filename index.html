Our Mission

Chasing sunsets & fast lines in Northern California

Bay Area Speed Skating Inc is a dedicated 501(c)(3) organization founded to remove physical and financial limits from speed skating. By coordinating closely with ice centers from Oakland to San Jose, we bring olympic-style speed skating to communities across California.

Through premium skate rental pools, expert instruction camps, and local time trials, we foster technical skill, self-confidence, sportsmanship, and raw athletic power.

"Speed skating combines precise balance, explosive power, and clean edge control. We believe any skater in NorCal should have a track to chase."
