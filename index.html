Why Train with BASS?

We train local athletes through community-supported tracks, providing safety gear, top-tier skate rentals, and Olympic-focused training protocols.

High-Performance Coaching

Structured regimens focusing on dry-land dynamic power, on-ice cornering, cross-over mechanics, and state-of-the-art endurance science.

Accessible Athletics

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we direct donations straight into equipment sponsorship pools and low-cost skate rentals for families.

California Community

Connecting families, active master skaters, and young speed demons across San Francisco, Oakland, and San Jose ice facilities.
